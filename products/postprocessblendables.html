Post Process Blendables - Volume 1

Post Process Blendables Volume 1 is a pack of 44 post process materials to give a variety of effects in your game..

OVERVIEW

Post Process Materials allow you to blend your existing Post process with shaders to create some interesting visual screen effects, like Blur, Night Vision, HDR, and many more. This pack comes with a set of 44 Materials with customizable parameters that allow you to give unique look to your Scene. You can blend multiple Post process material instances and set the parameters, to enhance the flexiblity of the outcome that matches your requirement.
